Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Disposable Exec collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the hosted execution service.

1. Information We May Collect

  • Account and contact information
  • Billing-related information provided through authorized billing providers
  • Usage and operational metadata, including API activity and execution-related logs
  • Security and abuse-prevention signals

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the service
  • To authenticate users and manage access
  • To enforce quotas, limits, and abuse-prevention controls
  • To monitor reliability, diagnose issues, and improve service quality
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Execution Data

Execution metadata and limited logs may be processed for operational, debugging, and security purposes. Customers should avoid submitting sensitive data unless they are comfortable with such operational processing.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with infrastructure vendors, billing providers, or service providers as necessary to operate the service, comply with law, or protect service integrity.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain records, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
